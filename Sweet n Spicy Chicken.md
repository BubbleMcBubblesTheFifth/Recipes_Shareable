# Ingredients

Chicken seasoning
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1.5 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon ground paprika
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for spiciness)
- ¼ teaspoon dried oregano

The other stuff
- 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
- 1 to 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1-2/3 cups tomato sauce
- 1/3 cup peach or apricot preserves
- Hot cooked rice

# Direction
1. Mix all the seasoning ingredients together. 
2. Add the chicken cubes and mix well until the chicken color changes. 
3. On a skillet, cook the chicken on med-high heat until almost cooked (about 5 minutes), stirring often. 
4. Turn the heat to low, add in the tomato sauce and preserves, and stir until incorporated. 
5. Cover and simmer for 2 minutes. 
6. Remove from heat and serve with rice.
