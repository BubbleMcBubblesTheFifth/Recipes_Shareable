
# ingredients

Dashi soup 
* 4 cup water
* 4 tsp dashi powder
Miso soup
* 7oz solf/silken tofu
* 4 Tbsp miso
* 1 Tbsp dried wakame seaweed
* 1 green onion/scallion

# Steps
1. Combine water + dashi powder over medium heat; bring to slow boil
2. Add 4 Tbsp miso paste; dissolve completely
3. Cut the tofu into 1/2 inch cubes 
4. Add 1 Tbsp wakame seaweed and chopped onions 
5. Serve immediately
