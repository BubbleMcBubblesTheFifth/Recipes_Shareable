# Ingredients 
1 lb bowtie pasta
1 lb Chicken (follow chicken alfredo recipe)
* 1/2 tsp of basil, salt, black pepper + 2 tbsp unsalted butter 
1/3 cup grated Parmesan
Spinach - add to hot noods or to pesto in saucepan  
3-4 cloves of minced garlic
8 oz mushrooms sliced
1/2 yellow onion diced
Barilla's creamy pesto sauce
A bunch of diced grape tomatoes
# Steps:
1. Boil water and add salt for noods
2. Add butter to pan and seasoned chicken. Saute on med-high until cooked (about 6 minutes).
3. Set aside and saute mushroom, onion, and garlic on medium heat until onion softens (about 3 minutes) 
4. Reduce heat to low and add pesto sauce, Parmesan, and spinach into pan. Stir until combined and spinach has wilted. 
5. Add pasta and chicken to pan. Stir well to combine. 
6. Add the diced tomatoes; toss to combine
7. Serve.
