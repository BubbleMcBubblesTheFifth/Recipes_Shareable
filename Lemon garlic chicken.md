### Ingredients

- 6 Chicken Thighs skin on (optional), boneless, cut into 1.5-inch pieces
- 2.5 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1.5 tablespoons oyster sauce
- 0.5 tablespoon dark soy sauce optional
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- Black pepper to season
- 1 lemon squeezed
- Random amount of mushrooms
- 1/2 red onion sliced
- 10-15 cloves garlic chopped
- 1 handful cilantro chopped

### Instructions

- Marinate chicken with soy sauce, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, honey, black pepper, and cornstarch. Set it aside for at least 5 minutes or when you prepare all the ingredients.
- In a pan, drizzle in oil, and turn the heat to medium-high. Pan fry the chicken for 6-7 minutes or chicken the chicken is slightly brown.
- Next, add in sliced onion and garlic, saute for 2-3 minutes or until onion has soften and become fragrant
- Turn the heat to high, and Finish off by adding in sliced lemon, and chopped cilantro, saute on high heat for 1 minute.
